I love to play football

what is football?
"Football" refers toa family of team sports centered on kicking a ball to score a goal, with "association football" (known as soccer in the US, Canada, and other regions) being the world's most popular form. In this version, two teams of 11 players use mostly their feet to move a spherical ball into the opponent's goal on a rectangular pitch, with the team scoring the most goals winning. The term "football" can also refer to other sports, such as American football, Canadian football, Australian rules football, and rugby, which are distinct codes with different rules and objectives.
