Who is Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born physicist, born in 1879, most famous for developing the theory of relativity and the mass-energy equivalence E=mcÂ². He also made key contributions to quantum theory, receiving the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect . Einstein, who later became a US citizen, emigrated from Germany in the 1930s due to the rise of the Nazi Party . He is widely regarded as one of the most influential scientists of the 20th century.
